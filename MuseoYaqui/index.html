Museo de los yaquis​

Located in Cocorit, Sonora, just north of the Ciudad Obregon, Museo de los Yaquis is dedicated to telling the Story of the Yaqui​.
Address: Gral. Álvaro Obregón 203, Centro, 85000 Ejido del Centro, Son., Mexico​

Cosmología

The 1st room of the Museo de los Yaquis is dedicated to Yaqui cosmology and how they came to be​.

Danzas

Throughout the Year the Yaquis have a variety of different dances during their festivals, Some of the most popular is the Deer Dancer and Pascola.

Instrumentos

Instruments play an important role to provide music for the Yaqui dances.

Exposiciones

This Museums provides multiple exhibit to drive you right into Yaqui culture and history
